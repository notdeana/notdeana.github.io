Artist Statement


My pieces from both 74 and 75 are reflective of personal and societal topics. From my interest in gaming, to more sensitive topics such as mental health, they show a more personal side of me through my art. Specifically my desire to go into game design, and my own needs in trying to talk about mental health since there is a push in trying to make it a norm.


These pieces revolve heavily around games I play such as dark souls and maplestory, and societal topics of mental health such as depression and anxiety.
